About Payroll Incorporated

Secure, Fast and Easy

Payroll Incorporated was founded in the year 2020 as a result of the chanllenges being faced by Ventures, Enterprises, small business owners and even small to big organization. Payroll came into existence as a solution to these problems faced by these business owners. We look at their challenges and profer a lasting, efficient and effective solutions to them.

Here are some of the features and benefits of our payroll system:

Accurate and Timely Payroll Processing: Our system ensures that your employees are paid accurately and on time, every time. We handle all aspects of payroll processing, from calculating taxes and deductions to processing direct deposits.


Streamlined Time Off Requests: Our system makes it easy for employees to request time off, and for managers to approve or deny those requests. Our system tracks employee vacation and sick time, and calculates their available time off based on company policies.


Centralized Employee Information: Our system provides a centralized location for employee information, including personal details, contact information, employment status, and job history. This makes it easy to manage your employee information in one place.

Easy Access to Reports: Our system provides various reports such as payroll reports, time off reports, and employee information reports. You can generate reports based on different parameters such as date range, employee, and department.


Get Started Today: If you're ready to simplify your payroll system and benefit management, sign up for our system today. Our system is easy to use, secure, and backed by our knowledgeable customer support team. Contact us today to learn more.
